Tourstock Inc. is an OTA based in New York USA, with offices in Hong Kong, Beijing and Shenzhen, China. We’re a team of travel-loving engineers and developers, specializing in providing Chinese travelers with all-around travel services for their overseas tours.

We are a team of travel-loving engineers, program and business developers. We are well-equipped with
expertise in e-commerce, network technology, and online-travel business. Most important of all, we understand
Chinese consumers and have extensive experiences and connections in the travel industry in China.


We “hand-pick” and “stock” travel services/products that are most
interesting to Chinese travelers, aiming to become the ultimate tour
warehouse for the ever growing number of Chinese travelers who
just love, love, and love to see the world.
